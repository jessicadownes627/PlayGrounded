Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 10, 2025

GoPlayThere (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in the GoPlayThere app and on our website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as names, emails, or phone numbers. The GoPlayThere app only uses your device’s location (with your permission) to show nearby playgrounds and indoor play spaces.

How We Use Location Data

Location data is used solely to display nearby parks and play areas. Your location is never stored, shared, or sold. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

GoPlayThere uses trusted mapping services such as Google Maps for location and display purposes. These services may collect anonymous, aggregated data to improve performance.

Children’s Privacy

GoPlayThere is designed for parents and families. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will appear on this page with a new “Effective Date.”

Contact

For questions or concerns, please contact us at goplaythere@gmail.com.
